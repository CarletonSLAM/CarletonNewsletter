IEEE Carleton Nemko Industry Tour
Date: Monday, Mar. 4, 2019
Time: 12:30 - 15:00
Location: 303 River Rd, Ottawa, ON K1V 1H2

IEEE Carleton is pleased to give you the opportunity to have an industry tour at a highly trusted and respected verification test facility, Nemko Ottawa.

The tour will take place at 12:30 AM on Monday March 4th. The tour will feature a pizza lunch with a presentation about the company, a walk through of the facility and multiple demo's of certification testing such as EMC testing, sound testing, ESD testing and more!

Register for the tour on the forum online or in the IEEE Carleton office, ME 3359. There is no payment required for this tour, it is free. There are 10 spots available.

CU AI Meetup Inauguration
Date: Wednesday Mar. 6, 2019
Time: 17:30 - 20:00
Location: TB 238

Come join us at our club's inauguration and learn more about the exciting field of Artificial Intelligence!

Since this is our first meeting, we would love to receive feedback on what they would like to see most out of this club.

The agenda for the event is:

1) Introduction to AI and the Club
2) Survey of what direction we should take the Club
3) A short demo on how to write a quick Python script that will get you dabbling quickly in Artificial Intelligence.

Date: Thursday Mar. 7, 2019
Time: 18:00 - 19:30
Location: ME 4463

IEEE WIE presents it's Speaker Series, an event where you get to hear from the women in the industry; their experience, their career paths, their goals, and their challenges faced.

To celebrate International Women's Day at Carleton University, IEEE WIE is hosting a Speaker Series featuring women panelists in the Engineering and Science industry!

Join us in this celebration to promote women in STEM!
