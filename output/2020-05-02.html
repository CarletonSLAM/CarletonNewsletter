Introduction to Project Management
Date: Tuesday March 3, 2020
Time: 6:00 PM - 7:30 PM
Location: ME 4463

This workshop will be talking about the importance of project management, through the experience the presenter in leading some projects at different companies such as how he improved agile processes to get his team to deliver an 8-month project in 4 months. In general, students will be learning methods to make their team more productive.

In addition, he will also talk about his experience in becoming a Project Manager and will also give tips to help prospective students wanting to follow that managerial career path.
