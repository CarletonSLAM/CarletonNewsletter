Volunteering Opportunities

Battle Royale XI Organizational Meeting

Date: Tuesday, October 10, 2017
Time: 7:30 PM - 8:30 PM
Location: SITE 4026, University of Ottawa

Battle Royale is a 24 hour LAN – Gaming competition held annually by the IEEE uOttawa, CarletonU, and Algonquin College Student Branches. It has been held at Algonquin College for a few years, and also previously at uOttawa and CarletonU. It is Ottawa’s largest LAN party involving various computer games and gaming console tournaments.

The goal of Battle Royale is to bring students and video game enthusiasts from across the Ottawa region together to share their passion for competitive gaming and game design while doing so for a good cause.

This event organization is ideal for anyone who’s interested in video game design, animation, getting experience in organizing hackathons/competitions or just playing video games! Although this event may seem heavy in the gaming section, this is a great event to not only develop management skills because you’re organizing 100+ competitors, but also experience how programming and hardware skills are applied in something we’ve grown up playing.

Find out more information about past Battle Royale events through the event website, Facebook event, and photo galleries

IEEE SPAC Organizational Meeting

Date: September 27th
Time: 7:45 PM - 8:45 PM
Location: MC 2014, Carleton University

The IEEE Student Professional Awareness Conference (SPAC) is an opportunity for students, academics, and professionals from industry to bridge the gap between the classroom and the boardroom and build professional connections.

SPAC is an annual formal dinner event which sees engineering and computer science students engaging with professionals within their fields. This event is organized the IEEE Carleton, IEEE uOttawa, and IEEE Algonquin branches.

Find out more information about past SPAC events through the event website, Facebook event, and photo galleries

This meeting will be covering the positions need filling for SPAC so come to this meeting if you are interested!

Hope to see you there!

Being part of our Branch Executive and Volunteer Team

We all volunteer our time and efforts to provide students and professionals multiple avenues to expose and drive innovation in the technical world.

The executives and volunteers at IEEE Carleton learn to become leaders. We create events and initiatives by: utilizing resources; coordinating with other student clubs and IEEE branches; and reaching out to professionals in the Industry and Academia. We all learn and teach each other how to work toward these goals throughout the year.

Interested in being part of the team? This opportunity allows you to experiement and improve you technical, communication, management, and leadership skills, receive credits in Carleton's Co-Curricular Record (CCR), and enchance your resume when the accomplishments you make at IEEE.

Come by our office (ME 3359) to get started!
