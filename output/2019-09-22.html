IEEE and SCESoc GitHub Workshop
Date: Tuesday September 24, 2019
Time: 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM
Location: CB 5109

Intro to Vivado 2016
Date: Saturday September 28, 2019
Time: 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM
Location: ME 4166

Vivado is a powerful toolchain in the world of digital design and one of the most widely used in Education and Industry.

In this workshop, we will explore Vivado, what it is, how it works and try some of its features. We will do some basic Verilog HDL, block design and going over how to properly version control your projects for GitHub!

The aim of this workshop is to help introduce students taking ELEC3500 to Vivado 2016 or anyone just interested in getting started with FPGA design. We will be working with one of the best entry-level FPGAs, the Nexys A7 100T (The same board to be used in ELEC3500)
