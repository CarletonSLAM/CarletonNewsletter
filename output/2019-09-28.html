IEEE Day Celebration
Date: Tuesday October 1, 2019
Time: 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM
Location: ME 4359

On October 1st 2019, we are celebrating IEEE Day!

So what is IEEE Day? It's a worldwide event, celebrating the first time in history when engineers worldwide and IEEE members gathered to share their technical ideas in 1884.

How are we celebrating at CU? FOOD MUSIC GAMES. Get to know execs, learn about IEEE, come socialize and have a good time!

**Join us on Tuesday Oct 1st, 6PM ME 4359. Bring some smiles and laughter! (And enough room for dessert)**
