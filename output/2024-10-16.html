Early Bird Tickets On-sale Now!

Why Should I Go?

The Student Professional Awareness Conference (SPAC) is an amazing oppurtunity for students to network and meet industry professionals in a semi-formal setting. It is a great way to get your foot in the door and learn more about the employment outlook in your desired field.
