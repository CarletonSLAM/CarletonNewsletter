Shopify Industry Tour 2018
Date: Monday, March 5, 2018
Time: 3:00 PM - 4:00 PM
Location: 150 Elgin Street

IEEE Carleton is pleased to give you the opportunity to get an inside look at one of the most popular establishments in the business world, Shopify.

Shopify stakes its claim at creating a platform for business owners to market their products and organize their merchandise to better suit their needs. Shopify helps businesses to manage things such as taking care of customer orders, selling online and in retail stores.

Join the IEEE Carleton's tour at Shopify at 3:00pm on Monday March 5th. The tour is to feature a walk through of Shopify's office, which is unique even compared to the most modern of offices.

Register for the tour at the IEEE Carleton office, ME 3359. A $5 fee will be required and the first 15 participants will be allowed to go. Cutoff date for entry is Sunday March 4th.

Transportation: OC Transpo bus 4 to Rideau.

Don't miss this special opportunity to visit a growing company in Ottawa and in North America!

Stay Late and Make Workspace
Date: Every Thursday
Time: 6:30 PM - 9:30 PM
Location: ME 4463, Carleton University

Have the perfect big idea, but never knew where to get started?

Want to play with new hardware, learn technologies, and experiment with hacking?

If you said yes to any of the above, this is the place for you! Stay Late and Make is an initiative by IEEE Carleton University to help you work on your personal projects. Our focus is on the tech to make your idea tick, and we're working on giving you the tools, funding, and the mentorship to make it happen.

Come join us every Thursday starting January 18th from 5:30 PM - 9:30 PM in ME4463 at Carleton University!

Be a part of our organizing teams!

We all volunteer our time and efforts to provide students and professionals multiple avenues to expose and drive innovation in the technical world.

Interested in being part of the team? This opportunity allows you to experiment and improve you technical, communication, management, and leadership skills, receive credits in Carleton's Co-Curricular Record (CCR), and enhance your resume when the accomplishments you make at IEEE.

Come by our office (ME 3359) or email us at info@ieeecarleton.ca
