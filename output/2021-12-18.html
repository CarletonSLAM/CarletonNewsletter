Nasa Systems Engineering: Introduction To Project Life Cycle And Future Developments In Space Exploration
Date: January 28, 2022
Time: 11:00 PM - 12:00 PM EST
Location: Online Event

Many engineers seeking to enter the space industry lack insight on project life cycle phases that may impede the project process and aptness to meet both requirements and deadlines. In this talk, we will go over the different phases that compose Project Life Cycle. This presentation will provide an overview of each phase, including: Concept Studies, Concept and Technology Development, Preliminary Design and Technology Completion, Final Design and Fabrication, System Assembly, Integration and Test, Launch, Operations and Sustainment, and Closeout. The presentation will conclude with an outlook of future technologies for space exploration and settlement. #engineering #ieee #youngprofessionals #projectlifecycle #technology #industry #NASA"

Intro to Neural Networks
Date: Wednesday January 19, 2022
Time: 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM EST
Location: Online Event

Deep Learning is the technology behind countless innovations in the past few years with applications in autonomous driving, biomedical informatics, image recognition and natural language processing. But what is deep learning, and how does it work? In this workshop, we will go over the foundational concepts of deep learning, particularly neural networks and how simple parts can build to form a complex system capable of modelling complex relationships.

Neural Networks Project 1: Building Your First Neural Network
Date: Thursday January 26, 2022
Time: 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM EST
Location: Online Event

Neural networks have taken over the world of artificial intelligence and machine learning, but how do you build one? Together in this hands-on workshop, we build a simple neural network that will make predictions based on tabular data. We will utilize state-of-the-art python libraries to build, train and test our neural networks.
